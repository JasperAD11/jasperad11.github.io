Basketball Vision

Every time I step on the court, time disappears—competition, team flow, and pure joy. That feeling has been with me since age five.

In 2023 at BEAT81, I met fellow hoopers Mikel and Tristen. Pickup games after work in Berlin, then Lisbon—rough courts, fading light, but no one wanted to stop. The game pulls you into flow.

The vision: a community hub built around love for the game—great energy, music, culture, and people. Add cardio and bodyweight training to make it accessible. Blend basketball with movement and create a space where time is meant to be lost—on purpose.

It fuses inclusivity, swag, music, and creativity. A playground for adults who want to play like kids—and kids who want to dream like pros. The idea is evolving and open to collaborators.

At its core: love for basketball, community, and the kind of joy that makes you forget the clock.
